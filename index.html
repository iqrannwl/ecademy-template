How does Ecademy work

Providing unparalleled access to three different levels of Race Pilot evolution, Ecademy bridges a quantum gap by providing complete novices the unique opportunity to become Formula racers in our one-stop-shop solution. Courses include Driving plus Classroom & Expert guidance.

Our inaugural Dubai IGNITE programmes launch early 2024. Simulators are widely used by today's current F1 drivers. They are a valuable tool for real world preparation. providing a controlled, safe and immersive environment. Fine-tune your racing lines, braking points, and cornering techniques on actual circuits you may race in future!
Race teams are constantly evolving and seeking bright new talent. As a developed racer, ADVANCE would see you transition to Formula cars with an opportunity to graduate to our parent Management company, Allstar International, who supported several drivers en-route to F1. Junior teams are also operated by leaders such as Red Bull & Mercedes.
